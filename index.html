More about Polygraph services

Polygraph: an instrument for receiving and recording simultaneously tracings of variations in certain body activities.

A polygraph exam is also referred to as a "lie detector" test. They use special technology to measure and at the same time, record, physiological chages, while the subject is answered a series of questions. Polygraph examinations or lie detector tests have become a cultural icon, used in everything from routine security screenings to high-profile investigations. While most people believe they are reserved strictly for government organizations or TV prgarms, lie detectors are actually available to help anyone interested in uncovering the truth. Why might one use a Polygraph service? It could be used for anything from Infidelity, Personal or business theft, family issues, pre-employment screenings, criminal investigations and more!
